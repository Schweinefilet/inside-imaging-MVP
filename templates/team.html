Cliff Oguk

Founder; Diagnostic Radiographer

The founder of Inside Imaging and a practicing Diagnostic Radiographer at a regional referral hospital in Kenya. The mission was born from a daily reality: handing patients complex medical reports and seeing the immediate wall of confusion and fear in their eyes. I founded this company not as a technologist from afar, but as a frontline healthcare worker who has witnessed the profound gap between a life-altering diagnosis and a patient’s ability to understand it. This firsthand experience is the bedrock of our platform—ensuring every feature is built with genuine empathy and clinical relevance. My passion extends beyond the hospital into advocacy and research, driving me to collaborate with radiographers across Africa. My role as a brand ambassador for a leading teleradiology platform further solidified my understanding of the systemic challenges we face. At Inside Imaging, I lead a team that shares a core belief: that the right to understand your own health is fundamental. We are combining deep clinical insight with cutting-edge technology to build a bridge of clarity for millions, transforming patient anxiety into agency and setting a new standard for equitable care.
