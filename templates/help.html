Frequently Asked Questions

What is Inside Imaging?

Inside Imaging is a secure platform that transforms complex medical imaging reports into simplified language that patients and non-specialists can easily understand.

How do I upload my radiology report?

After creating an account and logging in, navigate to the Dashboard page. Click on the "Upload Report" button, select your file, choose the modality type (CT Scan, MRI, X-ray, etc.), and follow the prompts to complete the upload process.

What languages are available for translation?

We currently offer simplified translations in English, Spanish, French, and Chinese. You can select your preferred language during the upload process on the Dashboard page.

How much does the service cost?

Our pricing is based on the type and complexity of the report. After uploading your report, you'll receive a quote before proceeding to payment.
