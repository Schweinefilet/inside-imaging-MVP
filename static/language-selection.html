Translation Sample

Below is an example of how medical terminology is translated into plain language in your selected language.

Original Radiology Text:

"Posterior-anterior and lateral views of the chest demonstrate no focal consolidation, pneumothorax, or pleural effusion. The cardiac silhouette is normal in size. The mediastinum and hilar contours are unremarkable."

Translated Plain Language:

"Front and side views of your chest X-ray show no signs of lung infection, collapsed lung, or fluid around your lungs. Your heart appears normal in size. The central chest structures and areas where major blood vessels enter the lungs look normal as well."
